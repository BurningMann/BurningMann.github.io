LAX Parking

LAX long-term parking rates range around $7-$17 per day. Garages inside the airport are capped at $40 per day, so an off-airport spot will save you a significant amount. Off-site economy lots start at $9 per day and indoor LAX valet parking starts as low as $12.75. The newest structure, Economy Lot E, is located off 111th Street, between Aviation and La Cienega boulevards. The daily rate at LAX Lot E is $12.

The best parking near LAX for dropping off or picking up passengers is at any of the garages located along World Way. These lots charge $4 for the first hour and then $4 for each additional hour or fraction thereof. There is a Cell Phone Waiting Lot located on W 96th street, where drivers and those waiting for you may arrive up to 2 hours early for free. Drivers are expected to wait in their vehicles and are not allowed to leave their vehicle unattended.

If you drive an oversized vehicle, Sunrise LAX Parking can accommodate you at $17 per day. Most car parking lots are located about a 7 to 15 minute shuttle ride from the terminals. The shuttles typically run every 10-20 minutes to and from the airport, contingent on traffic conditions. Most airport shuttle services operate 24/7 and are free with your reservation.

Reserve your LAX Parking

Always plan ahead and reserve a spot in advance when leaving a vehicle behind, especially during heavy travel weekends and holidays. There are several lots we recommend you reserve and pay for ahead of time so that on the day of your departure, parking and catching your flight will be the easiest part of your day.

Select from any of these parking lots for an easy and convenient parking reservation!
