About us

As a premier tent manufacturer in the USA, LUXMODULE excels in crafting top-quality tent structures. All our structures are meticulously produced in our dedicated tent factory, enabling us to offer customers optimal conditions in the shortest possible time. With representative offices in Europe and the CIS, and our headquarters located in Baku, we ensure a global presence.


The production of awning structures is carried out using Italian technology using high-quality materials, proven over years of work with strict quality control, guaranteeing excellent results for our clients. We provide reliable and stylish design solutions for events of any size, providing a wide choice to suit a variety of preferences and purposes.

Tent tents are ideal for this, as they are quickly assembled, harmoniously fit into any landscape, and are also cheaper than stationary buildings. Quickly erected tents are deservedly considered the optimal solution for a modern, dynamically changing business.

  • Individual approach to the project, taking into account all wishes, operating conditions and budget;
  • Materials and components of European quality from trusted suppliers;
  • Stylish design in any color scheme with the possibility of branding;
  • Reliability, safety and durability of tents;
  • Impeccable quality of workmanship;
  • Prompt delivery and installation anywhere in Belarus and beyond.
