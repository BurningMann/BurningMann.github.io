Any options for connecting structures


Wooden marquees Chalet can be linked together to create various shapes, and quadrodome tents of different sizes can also be interconnected. However, only structures with the same arched span can be connected, such as 6×6 with 6m arch or 8×8 with 8m arch size. The total area of these combined structures can reach several hundred square meters. It’s advisable to avoid arranging the tents in a square configuration, as this can create areas where moisture may accumulate.

Effortless Transportation


Our wooden tents are designed with logistics in mind, making transportation smooth and efficient. To help you plan ahead, we provide a detailed table with dimensions, capacity, and recommended vehicle types suitable for safe delivery. Whether you need a compact option for smaller setups or a larger transport solution for full-scale events, the guide ensures you can choose the right vehicle with confidence. This way, every step—from preparation to setup—remains simple, reliable, and stress-free.
